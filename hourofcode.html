Hour of Code

“An Hour of Code” is an event held once in a year to show people that everyone can learn basics under the slogan “Celebrate computer science everywhere!”. Everybody is able to host such an event anywhere they want. Everywhere more than 180 countries host an hour of code event. To contribute this event and help our students to learn about Computer Science ADA students planned meetings with students and as the 10th team (Data Pirates) we decided to go to secondary school number 54 in Baku. This is the school where our teammate, Shamir Valiyav, studied. We researched for basic information about Computer Science which is easy to understand for everyone. We created a power point presentation to display all things visually as well. In additionally, we are planning to give code.org certificates to the students to motivate them. If you want to learn more about this project, click here and go to official website.
